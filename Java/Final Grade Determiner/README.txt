In high school a lot of us would want to know what we'd have to get on the final exam in order to hit a target grade. This program would take our
4 marking period grades (we had no midterms), a desired grade, and would spit out a list of what you would get with all if you were to get a 60-100 on the final,
and then interlace that with what you would have to get to hit your target grade
